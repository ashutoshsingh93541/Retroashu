Pro Tip

The Hashtag Ladder

Stop spamming 30 tags. Start strategizing your reach.

Why the Old Method is Dead

Spamming massive hashtags like #gaming or #videogames will bury your post instantly. Millions of posts use those tags daily, pushing your reel to the bottom in seconds. You need to build a "ladder" that allows your content to rank in smaller communities before the algorithm pushes it to the massive ones.

The 3-5-2 Structure

Copy this exact ratio for your next upload.

3 Broad Tags

#GamingCommunity #PCSetup #CinematicGaming

5 Niche Tags

#GodOfWarPhotography #RX7600 #GamingDeskIdeas

2 Custom Tags

#RetroAshu #RetroAshuEdits

Noob vs. Pro Strategist

Noob Creator Pro Creator
Uses 30 generic tags (looks like spam) Uses 8 to 10 highly targeted tags
Uses tags with 10M+ posts Targets tags with 50K - 500K posts
Puts hashtags in the comments Integrates them naturally into the caption
